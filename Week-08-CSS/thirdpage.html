Shaolin Soccer (Chinese: 少林足球) is a 2001 Hong Kong sports comedy film directed by Stephen Chow, who also stars in the lead role.
A former Shaolin monk reunites his five brothers,[note 1] years after their master's death, to apply their superhuman martial arts skills
to play football and bring Shaolin kung fu to the masses.
